Who I am

My name is Lewey Geselowitz, originally from South Africa, I'm currently living in the US and working as the Lead Graphics and Interaction Programmer for HyperCube Inc. on their HyperChem molecular modeling and bioinformatics package. This is while pursuing a computer science degree at the University of Florida. I've also been a Software Design Engineer (programmer) intern at Microsoft (with the Avalon team, which is the user interface API for the next Windows). My main interests are artificial intelligence, computer graphics programming, new user interaction models, and art theory (pretty much what 'Godel, Escher, Bach' talks about). See my resume for my adventures in the world of computer science and the rest of the site for the stuff spoken of in there.

If you are interested in one of my projects and wish to work on it, port/compile it for a different platform, or just a comment or question about it, feel free to drop me a line at lewey@lewcid.com
