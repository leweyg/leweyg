Othello

Othello is just a simple implimenation of the classic board game. It contains a nice little AI which you can customize to a fair extent. Please note that I wrote this game a while ago, and was based of what I thought the rules were, it turns out the "official" rules of the game are slightly different (Oops!). Anyway, it's still fun to play around with. The instructions are included with the game.
