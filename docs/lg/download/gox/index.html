Gox - GO Hexogonally

Gox is GO Hexogonally. That is it's a Go board, except that most pieces have 6 neighbors instead of 4. This is a VERY rough implimentation, so please excuse any problems you may encounter. Also, while it will automatically removed killed stones, and it won't check for Ko violations. It will also only let you 'undo' one move. The + and - buttons adjust the number of stones per side, this is because I couldn't work out what the correct amount should be

Anyone wishing to have the source code, or just to shout at me for violating the perfect balance of Go may contact me at lewey@lewcid.com.
