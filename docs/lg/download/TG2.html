TG2
Connect 4 in 3D

TG2 is a great thinking game, which plays like a 3D version of Connect 4, you place balls on 16 polls arranged in a 4x4 pattern, and you try to get four of your balls in a row, two players alternate turns. This game can really lead to a lot of looking and some great fun. This program offers a free moving view, so you can look from any angle, I'm still working on the AI, but should have it soon, right now it's only 2 players.
