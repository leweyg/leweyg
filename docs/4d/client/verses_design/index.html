VERSES.IO - Designing for The Spatial Web

by Lewcid Systems


VERSES is a Spatial Web technology group championing HSTP (the Hyper Space Transaction Protocol).

Back in mid 2018, at the height of blockchain investment (which HSTP spatializes but doesn't require), I was deep in the development of 4D Interfaces... but something was missing, purely visualizing data somehow wasn't cutting it as a complete experience but I couldn't figure out why. It was then that a friend introduced me to VERSES's idea of "Spatial Contracts"; that a unified language could describe both the movements of things, movement of money, and the logical relationship and boundaries between those which we call trade, logistics, personal rights, privacy and even ecology itself. Society itself is really just a collection of spatial contacts.

I quickly flew to LA, interviewed and took a job with them as soon as possible, the role and design challenge? Design "a spatial browser": enabling users to spatial capture (tokenize) and contract (permission, sell, buy, etc.) the space around them, all on a never-before seen decentralized and spatially addressable platform.

Below are a number of videos and demos created during the early investigations. These led to the creation of VERSES QUICKSILVER and numerous other production grade interaction platforms powered by HSTP and VERSES's SWAN framework.
