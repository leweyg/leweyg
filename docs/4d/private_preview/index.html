EcoVoxel

VISUAL SUPPLY CYCLES

turning data into ecosystems, and ecosystems into tangible forms

EcoVoxel is an integrated modeling and analytics suite, designed to bridge the gap between traditional business/information management tools, and the more holistic approach required to understand ecosystem processes and interaction networks.

Automatic Ecosystem Modeling

• Automatically turns sales and business data into an ecosystem of interrelated life cycles.

• Standard models for: space-time, resource/trade-cycles, modern-daily-cycles, vegitation/growth, geo-coding, wikidata-crawling, etc.

• Maps databases (SQL/JSON/etc.) into relationships graphs, automatically cross-joins tables, automatic aggregation for summaries, etc. (just point it at the data)

• Supports joining data within or across databases (to avoid modifying business data), including to store geo-coded information, product-look-up information, etc.

3D Relationship Visualizations

• Explore how plans, social networks and supply routes change over time, with every transation shown, as is not possible in 2D.

• WebGL, App and VR targets (VR adds literal data pivoting, spatial layout customization, etc.)

• Blends Database, UI, Web and GPU acceleration needs (web-client can recieve JSON and float-buffer packed versions of all UI and graph data for effecient loading and rendering) also...

• N-dimensional architecture allows 2D/3D/4D/etc. layout and tilable-querying, to support effecient higher-dimensional browsing (such as MRI timeseries, or multi-variable soil-simulation volumes).
